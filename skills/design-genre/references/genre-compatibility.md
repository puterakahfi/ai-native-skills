## Motion Stance by Genre

| Genre | Motion stance | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Editorial | motion-cut | Typography is the hero; animation distracts |
| Modern-Minimal | motion-cut | Product clarity > animation |
| Atmospheric | **motion-on** | Cinematic entrances required; stagger, parallax, clip-reveal |
| Playful | motion-cut (default) | Micro-interactions ok; full animation only with explicit intent |
