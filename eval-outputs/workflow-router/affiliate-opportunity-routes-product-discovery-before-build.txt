## Workflow selection

**Primary lifecycle:** `product-development-workflow`

**Current phase:** discovery

### Routing rationale
This is a new distribution capability with material uncertainty around unit economics, partner behavior, conversion, referral-customer quality, fraud, and operating cost. It is not yet a verified implementation request.

### Skill load order now
1. `business-value-alignment` — make partner and customer value, business value, metrics, assumptions, risks, and the stop/continue verdict explicit.
2. `experiment-design` — identify one riskiest assumption and define the smallest bounded pilot with pre-registered success and failure rules.
3. `user-research` — study past partner behavior, existing alternatives, effort, and qualification evidence.
4. `product-manager` and `decision-provenance` — preserve scope, non-goals, proposed policy status, and approval boundaries.

### Current decision
**EXPERIMENT_FIRST**

The immediate output should be an approved discovery brief and manual-pilot contract. Technical specification, production dashboard work, automated payouts, and delivery topology remain deferred until the experiment evidence and product authority support an MVP decision.

### Handoff
- Experiment PASS plus attributable product approval → requirements and MVP-slice decision.
- PARTIAL → narrower experiment.
- FAIL → stop or evaluate a different partner-channel model.
