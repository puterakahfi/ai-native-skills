## Value alignment

**Verdict: EXPERIMENT_FIRST**

### User value
Potential partners gain a measurable way to monetize useful tutorials, reviews, and workflow demonstrations. Referred customers gain contextual education and proof of use before purchasing.

### Business value
The opportunity could create performance-based acquisition, qualified revenue, product education, and learning about the most convertible customer segments. These benefits are plausible, but not yet established by product evidence.

### Evidence labels

**KNOWN**
- The product is sold once rather than through a recurring subscription.
- An affiliate channel would require attribution, commission rules, and partner operations.

**ASSUMED**
- Relevant creators will publish useful content when offered a commission.
- Referred customers will be sufficiently qualified to reach first value.
- A small content kit will reduce partner activation friction.

**UNKNOWN**
- Sustainable commission range after payment fees, tax, support, refunds, and fraud.
- Which partner segment converts best.
- Click-to-purchase conversion and referred-customer activation quality.
- Operational cost and dispute rate.

### Metrics
- **Leading:** qualified partner interviews, approved pilot partners, percentage publishing within seven days, time to first content, and referral clicks.
- **Lagging:** valid paid transactions, net contribution after commission, percentage of productive partners, and referred-customer activation.
- **Guardrail:** refund and chargeback rate, misleading claims, spam complaints, support cost, attribution disputes, and customer-data exposure.

### Riskiest assumption
Qualified partners will invest the effort to create and publish useful content when the expected commission per transaction may be modest.

### Recommended first step
Run a bounded manual pilot with a small curated cohort. Pre-register success and failure thresholds, verify unit economics, and measure customer activation—not only clicks or stated interest. Do not authorize a full self-service platform until the evidence supports it.
